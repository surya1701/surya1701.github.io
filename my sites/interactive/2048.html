Introduction

This is an interactive story based in the year 2048. The world has changed and CyberLife Inc. is leading this new age. They introduced the world to Androids, synthetic beings that resemble human appearance and are built to serve humans. Androids are now found in nearly every household, thanks to their low price along with the ability to perform any given task.

You are Connor, the first ever detective android. You have been asked to go to the 10th floor of Alpine Apartments as a negotiator. It has been reported that for the first time ever an android has become a deviant. It has a gun and threatens to jump off the roof with a 10 year old girl. There have been casualties, so act fast. The girl's life is in your hands, make the right choices.
